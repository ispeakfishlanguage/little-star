Little Stars

Dance, Sing, Shine - Always Free, Forever Fun!

Welcome to Little Stars!

We are a dancing and singing club for kids aged 3 to 5, funded by the municipality of the city of Stockholm.

Discover our story, learn about our commitment to the arts, and see why Little Stars is the perfect place for your little one.

At Little Stars, we see the potential in every child. Dancing and singing aren't just activities; they're paths to confidence, discipline, and lasting memories.

Because we deeply value children and the arts, Little Stars is completely free. We believe that every young talent should have a stage, without any cost.

Our Classes

Each class at Little Stars is carefully tailored to meet the energetic and imaginative needs of our tiny dancers and singers. We warmly invite parents and grandparents to be a part of this joyful journey!

For Age 3

Perfectly designed for the youngest of our stars, this class introduces them to basic rhythms and movements, sparking their creativity. Weekdays: 4pm - 5pm

For Age 4

Building on foundational skills, our 4-year-old stars delve deeper into dance routines and start vocal training. Weekdays: 5pm - 6pm

For Age 5

For our eldest stars, this class offers advanced routines and vocal lessons, nurturing their growing talents. Weekdays: 6pm - 7pm

Note: Parents and grandparents are encouraged to participate and support their little stars during classes.
