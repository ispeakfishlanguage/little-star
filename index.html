About us

Welcome to Little Stars!

We are a dancing and singing club for kids aged 3 to 5, funded by the municipality of the city of Stockholm.


Learn about our commitment to the arts, and see why Little Stars is the perfect place for your little one.


Nestled in the vibrant heart of Stockholm is "Little Stars", a dance and singing club uniquely tailored for the budding talents aged 3 to 5. Unlike any other initiative, "Little Stars" is more than just an ordinary club—it's a journey designed to foster creativity, passion, and the boundless potential of our next generation. Each melody and dance step taken in our program is a leap towards confidence, discipline, and a treasury of memories that will span lifetimes.

Our ethos at "Little Stars" goes beyond mere artistry; it's a commitment. We believe that no child should be left behind in the exploration of the arts, which is why our doors are open to all, free of charge. As the rhythm of music and dance reverberates within our walls, we're not just creating artists; we're paving the path for future leaders, thinkers, and dreamers. So, to parents looking to kindle their child's artistic spark and to young stars ready to shine, "Little Stars" beckons as your luminous beginning to an illustrious journey.

Our club isn't just about the arts; it's a pledge for a brighter future — always free, forever fun.

Our Classes

Each class at Little Stars is carefully tailored to meet the energetic and imaginative needs of our tiny dancers and singers. We warmly invite parents and grandparents to be a part of this joyful journey!

For Age 3

Perfectly designed for the youngest of our stars, this class introduces them to basic rhythms and movements, sparking their creativity.
Weekdays: 4pm - 5pm

For Age 4

Building on foundational skills, our 4-year-old stars delve deeper into dance routines and start vocal training.
Weekdays: 5pm - 6pm

For Age 5

For our eldest stars, this class offers advanced routines and vocal lessons, nurturing their growing talents.
Weekdays: 6pm - 7pm

Note: Parents and grandparents are encouraged to participate and support their little stars during classes.
